Academics

Baduriya Central College provides education from Grade 1 to Grade 13. Our goal is to guide students at every stage of learning and prepare them for higher education and life.

Grade Levels

  • Primary (Grades 1–5): Building strong basics in reading, writing, and math.
  • Secondary (Grades 6–11): Preparation for the O/L examination.
  • Advanced Level (Grades 12–13): Students choose from the streams below.

A/L Streams

  • Arts Stream
  • Commerce Stream
  • Biology Stream
  • Combined Maths Stream
  • Technology Stream

Extracurricular Activities

Our students take part in many activities such as:

  • Educational Trips
  • Sports Meets & Football Tournaments
  • Old Boys’ and Old Girls’ Events
  • Various School Competitions
